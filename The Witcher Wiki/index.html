Some information about The Witcher:

The Witcher is a series of fantasy short stories and five novels about the witcher Geralt of Rivia. Written by award wininng Polish Author Andrzej Sapkowski. The books have been adapted into a movie and two television series (The Hexer and Netflix's The Witcher), a video game series (The Witcher), a comic book and others. The novel series (excluding the short stories) is also called the Witcher Saga or the Blood of the Elves saga.
